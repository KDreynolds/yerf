I’m Kyle Reynolds, a QA contractor who has led quality for multiple products — mobile apps and web platforms — all at the same time. I go from manual UAT sessions straight through to writing complete end-to-end test suites from scratch.

I specialise in test automation (Playwright, Cypress, XCTest and Maestro) across mobile and web, designing pragmatic QA processes, and making sure nothing slips through the cracks — no matter how many products are in flight.

When I’m not breaking software on purpose, I contribute to open-source testing tools and write about quality engineering best practices.
